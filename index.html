The Big Five

In the context of wildlife, the 'Big Five refers to the five most difficult animals in Africa to hunt on foot, but it is now more widely used by game viewing tourists and safari tour operators

In Africa, the Big Five game animals are the lion, leopard, rhinoceros, elephant, and African buffalo.
